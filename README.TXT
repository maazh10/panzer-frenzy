USER INSTRUCTIONS:

-Move the tank around with arrow keys. 
-Shoot bullets by pressing space
-Kill the enemies coming from different directions to earn points. (10 to win)
-If you get hit by an orange tank you lose life and if you get hit by a black tank you die immediately.
-If you have one life left you can pick up health which appears on random spots.
-If you are low on bullets you can pick reloader which appears at random spots too. (total 30 bullets are given)
-You can pick up fire bullet powerup to get a flaming faster bullet for a while.
-You can pick up shield to get protected from enemies for a while.
